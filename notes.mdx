### 10-Minute Script: "Mastering Household Electricity Costs with Precision" 

---

**Slide 1: Introduction**  
**Speaker Notes:**  
"Let’s face it: electricity bills can be a real mystery. But what if I told you there’s a smarter way to take control? Today, I’ll introduce you to an app tailored specifically for India, designed to calculate household electricity bills with precision and even help you save energy. This isn’t just about bills—it’s about making informed choices to save money and the environment."

---

**Slide 2: The Problem**  
**Speaker Notes:**  
"Managing electricity expenses can feel like solving a complex puzzle. Every room, every appliance, and every light adds to the cost, but most of us never get to see the breakdown. That’s where this app comes in—a clear, customizable solution that breaks down your consumption by room, showing you exactly where your money is going."

---

**Slide 3: App Features**  
**Speaker Notes:**  
"This app is packed with features that make it a game-changer:  
1. **Room-Specific Breakdown**: Enter wattage and usage frequency for each room. You’ll see where your electricity usage is highest—be it the living room, kitchen, or bedroom.  
2. **India-Specific Rates**: The app uses the latest electricity rates and tariffs for precise calculations, so no guessing games!  
3. **AI-Powered Tips**: It doesn’t stop at calculations. The app suggests actionable steps to reduce energy consumption and your bills."

---

**Slide 4: Cost Breakdown Example**  
**Speaker Notes:**  
"Let’s take a closer look at room-based electricity costs:  
- The **Living Room** takes the biggest chunk—25%, which includes TV, lighting, and air conditioning.  
- **Kitchens** use about 15%, covering essentials like refrigerators and stoves.  
- **Bedrooms** and **Bathrooms** follow with smaller portions.  
By knowing this breakdown, you can easily identify where to cut back."

---

**Slide 5: Daily and Monthly Calculations**  
**Speaker Notes:**  
"Here’s how the costs play out daily and monthly:  
- The **Living Room** costs INR 15 per day or 450 per month.  
- The **Kitchen** costs INR 9 per day or 270 per month.  
And so on. These numbers are real insights—no more surprise bills."

---

**Slide 6: Behind the Tech**  
**Speaker Notes:**  
"Now, the tech enthusiasts might be curious—what powers this app? It’s built with:  
- **Next.js**: For a fast, responsive front end.  
- **TypeScript**: Ensures robust and maintainable code.  
- **Gemini**: Drives the AI smarts behind personalized energy-saving tips.  
This stack ensures you get a modern, reliable experience."

---

**Slide 7: Energy-Saving Tips**  
**Speaker Notes:**  
"Beyond just calculations, this app helps you optimize energy usage with simple but effective tips:  
1. Turn off lights and appliances when not in use.  
2. Replace traditional bulbs with energy-efficient LEDs.  
3. Unplug devices to avoid phantom power consumption.  
4. Invest in energy-efficient appliances with high energy star ratings.  
Small changes, big savings!"

---

**Slide 8: Conclusion**  
**Speaker Notes:**  
"To sum up: this app isn’t just a tool; it’s your partner in managing household electricity costs. By breaking down your usage, offering savings tips, and adapting to Indian tariffs, it empowers you to take control. The next step? Give it a try and see how much you can save—not just money, but also energy for a greener tomorrow. Thank you!"
